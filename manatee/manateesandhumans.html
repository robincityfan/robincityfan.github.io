Manatees and Humans

Manatees were hunted for their meat and oil a few decades ago.

Today manatees are injured by boats and sometimes killed. A lot of manatees have these scars.

Manatees are vulnerable to extinction

You can see Manatees in the folowing zoos and safari parks:

  • Lowry Zoo
  • Miami Seaquarium
  • SeaWorld Orlando
  • Colombus Zoo
  • Cincinnati Zoo
  • Teirpark Berlin
  • Nuremberg Zoo
  • Zooparc de Beauval
  • Aquarium of Genoa
  • River Safari Singapore

Manatees have been used to keep some waterways from being clogged up with aquatic plants
